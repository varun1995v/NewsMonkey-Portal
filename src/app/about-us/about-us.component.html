HISTORY

NewsMonkey was founded by a pair of over-energetic individuals who swore to the world they'd explore the entire sea and return triumphantly with the juiciest of news. As much as the society looked upon their conquest with cold eyes, they defied the odds. Or so was the tale they tell to the media if interviewed today. In reality, they just started off like every other company - with a proper plan, and funding of course.
